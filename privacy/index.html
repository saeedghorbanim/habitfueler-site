Privacy Policy for HabitFueler

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Introduction

HabitFueler ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application HabitFueler (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

Water Intake Data:

  • Daily water consumption records you manually log in the App
  • Water intake goals and targets you set
  • Time and date stamps of when you log water intake
  • Glass size preferences and measurement units

Reminder Preferences:

  • Notification schedules you configure
  • Reminder frequency settings
  • Preferred reminder times throughout the day
  • Do Not Disturb periods you set

Account Information (if applicable):

  • User preferences and app settings
  • Device notification permissions

Information Automatically Collected

Device Information:

  • Device type and model (e.g., iPhone 15, iPad Pro)
  • Operating system version (iOS version)
  • App version information
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Time zone and locale settings

Usage Data:

  • Features you use within the App
  • Time and date of your app access
  • App performance data and crash reports
  • Interaction patterns with notifications and reminders

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide App Functionality: Track your daily water intake and display your hydration progress
  • Send Reminders: Deliver scheduled notifications to remind you to drink water throughout the day
  • Personalize Experience: Customize reminder schedules based on your preferences and habits
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features, fix bugs, and improve user experience
  • Future Features: Enable upcoming nutritional reminder features
  • Provide Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide technical assistance

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All water intake data you enter is stored locally on your device
  • Your hydration records are NOT transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers
  • Your water intake information remains private and under your control on your device
  • Data persists until you delete the app or manually clear your records

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Industry-standard encryption for data storage
  • Secure coding practices
  • Regular security assessments
  • iOS platform security features (Keychain, secure storage)

However, please note that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

How We Share Your Information

We Do NOT Sell Your Personal Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Currently, HabitFueler does not share data with third-party services. Your water intake data remains entirely local on your device.

Apple Inc.:

  • Purpose: Deliver push notifications for water reminders
  • Information Shared: Device token for notifications only (no personal data)
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Future Integrations

If we introduce features that require data sharing (such as Apple Health integration), we will:

  • Update this Privacy Policy
  • Request your explicit permission
  • Clearly explain what data is shared and why
  • Allow you to opt-out at any time

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

Notifications and Reminders

How Notifications Work

HabitFueler uses iOS push notifications to remind you to drink water:

  • Local Notifications: Reminders are scheduled locally on your device
  • No Server Required: Notifications do not require internet connection
  • User Control: You can enable, disable, or customize reminders at any time
  • System Permissions: Managed through iOS Settings → Notifications → HabitFueler

Opting Out

You can disable notifications at any time:

  1. In-App: Settings → Notifications → Turn off reminders
  2. iOS Settings: Settings → Notifications → HabitFueler → Toggle off

Your Privacy Rights

Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: All your water intake data is accessible within the App
  • Modify Your Data: Edit or update any water intake records directly in the App
  • Delete Your Data: Remove individual records or clear all data within the App settings
  • Export Your Data: View your hydration history and statistics

Data Deletion

To permanently delete all your data:

  1. Open HabitFueler → Settings → Clear All Data, or
  2. Uninstall the App from your device (this removes all locally stored data)

Children's Privacy

HabitFueler is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

For Parents/Guardians:

  • All data is stored locally on the device
  • No account creation or personal information is required
  • Notifications can be managed through iOS parental controls
  • If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights regarding your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request information about the personal data we collect
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: You have the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (Note: We do not sell personal information)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: You have the right to not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

International Data Transfers

Your information, including personal data, may be stored on devices located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where data protection laws may differ.

HabitFueler stores data locally on your device. If you use the app while traveling internationally, your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to servers in other countries.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy

Your continued use of the App after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
