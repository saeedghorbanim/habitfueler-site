Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my water intake?

Tracking water is simple and quick:

  1. Open HabitFueler on your iPhone
  2. Input your general information
  3. Select your time zone and notification periods
  4. Tap "Drink a glass" to confirm intake

Your daily progress gets updated by you, showing you how much water you've consumed and how close you are to your daily goal.

How do I set up water reminders?

Stay on track with customizable reminders:

  1. Open the app and go to Settings
  2. Tap "Schedule"
  3. Enable "Sound" toggle
  4. Set your schedule:
    • Choose reminder times throughout the day
  5. Save your preferences

Note: Make sure notifications are enabled for HabitFueler in your iOS Settings: Settings → Notifications → HabitFueler → Allow Notifications

Can I customize my daily water goal?

Yes! Personalize your hydration target:

  1. Go to Settings in the app
  2. Find "Schedule"
  3. Enter your target in glasses per day
  4. Save

Recommended: Most adults should aim for 8 glasses (64 ounces or 2 liters) per day, but your needs may vary based on activity level, climate, and health conditions.

Can I edit or delete a water entry?

Made a mistake? No problem:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Choose:
    • Reset Today's Progress or Reset All Data

Will my data sync across devices?

Currently, HabitFueler stores all data locally on your device:

  • Your water intake records stay on your iPhone
  • Data does not automatically sync between devices
  • This ensures your privacy and works offline

Future Updates: We're exploring iCloud sync for future versions, which would allow you to access your data across all your Apple devices.

Does HabitFueler work offline?

Yes! HabitFueler works completely offline:

  • ✅ Track water intake without internet
  • ✅ Receive reminders without connection
  • ✅ All features available offline

No internet or cellular connection required!

Is my water intake data private?

  • ✅ All data stored locally on your device
  • ✅ No cloud servers or databases
  • ✅ Not shared with third parties
  • ✅ Not sold or used for advertising
  • ✅ You have full control

For complete details, review our Privacy Policy.

Troubleshooting

I'm not receiving reminder notifications

  • Make sure Do Not Disturb is off
  • Add HabitFueler to allowed apps during DND

4. Focus Modes:

  • Check iOS Focus settings
  • Allow HabitFueler notifications during Focus

My daily goal isn't showing correctly

Try these steps:

  1. Check your goal setting: Settings → Schedule → Verify the number is correct
  2. Restart the app: Close HabitFueler completely and reopen to refresh

The app crashed or froze

Quick fixes:

  1. Force close the app: Swipe up from bottom (or double-click Home button), swipe up on HabitFueler to close, then reopen
  2. Restart your device: Hold Side button + Volume button, slide to power off, turn back on
  3. Update the app: App Store → Updates → Update HabitFueler if available
  4. Still having issues? Email us at habitfuelera@gmail.com
    • Include: iPhone model, iOS version, description of issue
